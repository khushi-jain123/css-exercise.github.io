Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Butter
  • White bread slices

Steps you have to follow!

1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy. Remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.

2. Butter one side of each bread slice.

3. On the non-buttered side of a bread slice, place a slice of cheddar cheese, followed by bacon slices and tomato slices. Top with another slice of bread, buttered side facing out.

4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.

5. Remove from skillet, slice, and serve hot.
